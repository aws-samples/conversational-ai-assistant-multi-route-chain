Device 1004 Industrial Worksite Environmental Monitor  

Overview
Device 1004 is a heavy-duty environmental sensor designed to track critical environmental conditions in demanding industrial worksite applications.

1. Features
- Rugged housing withstands vibrations, shocks, heat
- Monitors temperature, humidity, air quality
- Alarm triggers for unsafe gas levels  
- Explosion-proof model available 
- Wireless connectivity up to 1500m

2. Technical Specifications 
- Temperature Range: -40°C to 120°C
- Humidity Range: 0% to 100% RH 
- VOC Range: 0 to 50 ppm
- Particulate Range: 0 to 1000 μg/m3
- Wireless Connectivity: Up to 1500m

3. Installation Guide
- Mount securely in representative area
- Integrate with worksite monitoring and alarm systems
- Calibrate and test response prior to full operation

4. Maintenance and Troubleshooting
- Periodically clean housing and inspect  
- Check battery and connectivity status
- Contact technical support as needed

5. Safety Guidelines
- Install explosion-proof model in hazardous areas
